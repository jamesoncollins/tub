Calibrated Coil Area Scaling

Reasoning: anchor to the flue-coil dataset (about 19 lb wood, 33% efficiency, 50 ft x 1/2 in copper), then scale by both wood feed rate and coil area ratio. First-pass model: Q_scaled ~= Q_base * (feed2/feed1) * (A2/A1), with A = pi * D * L. Final usable power is capped by fuel power at target feed: Q_usable = min(Q_scaled, feed2 * BTU_per_lb * eff). Source: new test page.
